    FAQs

    Find answers to your questions about our programs, schedules, and enrollment process.

    Is Arena STEM for education or entertainment?

    Both! We are a one-of-a-kind facility where kids and families come for STEM educational programs and also hands-on play in our 3,000+ sq foot Arena with the latest technology and real sized Robots!

    We also do birthdays, events, and school trips!

    Can I bring my children just to play?

    Yes, drop in 5PM to 8 PM weekdays to play in our Arena. It costs $15/child/hour. You can also buy our monthly access membership (month-to-month, no commitments) and have unlimited access!

    What does the monthly access include? How much is it?

    The monthly membership includes many awesome perks for just $169/month! Please check our Pricing page for details. But the main benefit of a monthly access is:

    • One STEM class per week (your choice), followed by playtime in Arena.
    • Unlimited access to the Arena for playtime on any other day.
    What STEM classes do you offer?

    We offer a variety of STEM-related classes. The most popular is our proprietary Roadmap Program, where we cover a new STEM topic every 3 weeks. We usually start this program for kids 7 years and older. Younger kids can start with our Lego Robotics classes. Please review all our amazing classes on Education page.

    How long is each class? When do they start?

    Each class runs for approximately 1 hour, and it is followed by 1 hour of playtime in our open arena. Kids will spend at least 2 hours with us per evening. All classes start at either 5PM or 6 PM every day, and you can do 1 hour of arena playtime before or after class. We also offer Saturday classes.

    Can my child play in the arena even on days they don't have class?

    Yes, with a monthly access membership, your child can come and enjoy the arena on any day, even if they don't have a class scheduled.

    How do I sign up for a membership?

    You can sign up for a monthly membership through our Pricing page. We only have 50 spots available.

    Is there a limit to how many classes my child can attend each week?

    The monthly membership includes access to one class per week. If you would like your child to attend more than one class per week, please contact us for additional options.

    Can I cancel my monthly membership at any time?

    Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. There is no long term commitment. Please note that refunds may not be available for partial months.

    Do you offer trial classes?

    Yes, our first class is always free. It’s a great way to check out the facility and try our classes. Please contact us to schedule a trial session for your child.

    Still have questions?

    Don’t hesitate to ask us or enroll in a free trial lesson to see everything for yourself!
