    Level 1

    STEM Explorer

    This course offers a great insight into what the future of STEM holds.
    The program includes, but is not limited to, the following topics:

    • 3D Printing

      A practical introductory course into the world of 3D printing. Kids will gain hands-on experience working with a 3D pen and learn CAD software to design their own 3D models. They will also print their designs on a real 3D printer!

    • Engineering Wizards

      A fascinating introduction to the mechanical and engineering world. Kids will learn about simple mechanisms and how they work by assembling them using building blocks.

    • Drones

      One of the most exciting topics for the future. During this class, kids will have the opportunity to learn how to operate a basic drone, build their own drone, and create operating code for a programmable drone.

    • Scratch Coding Cat

      A block programming course based on Scratch. Over three classes, kids will practice coding real-world objects and create their own game.

    • Volts Voyage Basics

      An introduction to the world of low voltage. Kids will learn to create basic electronic circuits and operate switches, motors, and lights.

    Additional included workshops:

    • Wonder Workshop Dash & Dot

      A captivating course featuring Dash & Dot learning robots. Kids will tackle real-world programming challenges and create a self-driving robot.

    • Microworld

      A journey into the unseen world, beyond what the human eye can perceive.

    • Chemistry Magic

      Unforgettable and amazing experiments in chemistry. From generating electricity using potatoes to boiling liquids with their hands, kids will experience the wonders of chemistry.

    • Woodwork Hands-On Building

      With basic woodworking tools, kids will create something cool that they can take home.

    Level 2

    STEM Pioneer

    This is the next step after the Explorer roadmap, offering kids deeper insights into well-known topics and introducing them to new ones.
    7+ years old.

    • Advanced 3D Printing

      Enhance 3D skills by creating complex models and structures, using real-world measurements, and designing multi-part models. Kids will also be introduced to other CAD software like Lego Studio.

    • Lego Powered by Scratch

      Whether it’s controlling real-world objects with code or using Lego blocks to control code, kids will explore these exciting possibilities in this course.

    • Drones

      Building on flight skills and delving deeper into different types of drones, including FPV (First Person View) drones.

    • Financial Literacy

      Our award-winning program focuses on building an entrepreneurial mindset and good money habits in kids.

    • Robots A2Z

      Programming life-sized humanoid robots is always a fun challenge. Kids will have the opportunity to develop this skill exclusively at Arena STEM.

    • AI Journey

      AI is undeniably essential for the future. Kids will learn about different types of AI and use cases, and they’ll even create their own neural network to recognize objects using a camera.

    Additional included workshops:

    • Tamiya Racing Cars

      Enter the exciting world of racing cars. Kids will build their own race car and enhance it by upgrading parts such as the motor, wheels, body, and suspension system.

    • Building Engino Cars

      Create advanced sensor-driven cars using Engino sets. Kids will learn to operate real-world sensors and engage in Arduino programming in a fun and interactive way.
